How I Work

Modern Workflow

A structured, agile process that balances discovery with delivery — adaptable across 5-day sprints and multi-month product cycles alike.

01

Discovery & Insights

User research, stakeholder interviews, heuristic reviews, and problem framing — using both qualitative insight and quantitative analytics to define the right problem before designing a solution.

02

Ideation & Exploration

Sketching, concept generation, and rapid ideation — often facilitated through Design Sprints or collaborative workshop formats to generate and align on direction quickly.

03

Design & Prototype

From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, Adobe XD, or Framer — with WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility baked in from the start, not bolted on at the end.

04

Testing & Iteration

Usability testing with real users, rapid iteration based on findings, and continuous feedback loops — validated against both qualitative feedback and behavioural analytics.

05

Implementation & Measurement

Working alongside development teams through delivery — writing detailed specs, reviewing builds against design, and tracking measurable outcomes post-launch using analytics tools.

About Me

Designing with purpose

I'm a UX/UI Product Designer with over 5 years of experience building scalable digital products that solve real user problems and drive business results — across B2B SaaS, consumer mobile apps, public-sector services, and luxury retail.

My work is grounded in user research and analytics, and I design with accessibility at the core — applying WCAG 2.2 AA standards and inclusive design principles as a matter of practice, not compliance. I'm experienced working within GDS frameworks and the GOV.UK Design System, and have delivered in regulated environments where rigour and clarity are non-negotiable.

I believe great design lives at the intersection of craft and collaboration. Whether it's a 5-day design sprint or a 12-week product overhaul, I bring strategic thinking, technical awareness (HTML, CSS, JS), and close partnership with development teams to deliver experiences that hold up in the real world.

I'm always keeping pace with emerging tools and methods — from AI-assisted design workflows to new accessibility standards — and I'm currently open to full-time and freelance opportunities.
