Case Study · 10 Weeks · Product Designer

MindNest

Redesigned the onboarding and habit formation experience for a wellness app — achieving a 35% reduction in week-one churn and a 22% increase in daily active users.

Timeline
10 Weeks
Role
Product Designer
Tools
Figma · Miro · UXPilot
Team
PM · 2 iOS Eng · Content

The Problem

52% of users churned within the first 7 days before experiencing the app's core value proposition. New users felt overwhelmed by options and unclear about how to build a consistent wellness routine.

01

Discovery & Insights

Analysed cohort data to identify churn patterns. Conducted diary studies with 8 users over 2 weeks. Found that users who set reminders had 3× better retention — a key insight that shaped the entire redesign direction.

02

Ideation & Exploration

Explored habit formation frameworks (BJ Fogg, Nir Eyal). Sketched 15 different onboarding approaches and ran collaborative ideation sessions. Prioritised a progressive goal-setting model based on user research findings.

03

Design & Prototype

Created personalised onboarding flows based on user goals. Designed a streak system with gentle, timely reminders. Built an interactive iOS prototype in Figma complete with micro-animations to validate with users.

04

Testing & Iteration

Ran a beta test with 50 users over 2 weeks. Tracked daily engagement and task completion rates. Simplified the goal-setting flow significantly after testing revealed confusion around multi-step input screens.

Outcomes

–35%
Week-one churn rate
+22%
Daily active users
63%
Retention (up from 48%)
