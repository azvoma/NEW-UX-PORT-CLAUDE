Case Study · 8 Weeks · UI/UX Designer

Harrods Pre-Order Feature

Designed a luxury-grade pre-order experience allowing customers to reserve high-demand products before official release — reducing frustration, improving demand forecasting, and earning an 85% reduction in related support queries.

Timeline
8 Weeks
Role
UI/UX Designer
Tools
Figma · Sketch · Miro · HTML/CSS/JS
Team
PM · Tech Lead · QA Engineer

The Problem

Harrods had no pre-order capability. High-intent customers missed limited-edition products entirely, leading to frustration and lost conversion. There was no mechanism to capture demand before launch, forcing customers to repeatedly check availability — a pattern incompatible with the premium Harrods experience.

01

Research

Used a mixed-method approach: qualitative interviews with Harrods customers across segments, user journey analysis of limited-edition purchase flows, competitive analysis of luxury pre-order patterns, and an analytics review of existing conversion data. Key finding: customers valued guaranteed access and transparency above all else.

02

Persona & Problem Framing

Anchored design decisions around a high-value luxury shopper persona — someone for whom shopping should feel effortless and exclusive. The core design challenge was making pre-order feel like a luxury service, not a standard ecommerce feature.

03

Ideation & User Flows

Generated concepts using Crazy 8s and rapid sketching, evaluated against user impact, technical feasibility, WCAG 2.2 AA compliance, and brand alignment. Designed end-to-end user flows ensuring the pre-order journey felt familiar, integrated, and free of disruptive standalone processes.

04

Design & Prototype

Started with low-fidelity wireframes in Figma to validate flows and hierarchy — tested terminology clarity and accessibility including screen reader compatibility — then moved to high-fidelity mobile-first screens. Collaborated closely with the development team throughout, ensuring designs were feasible in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Final Screens

"Andrew brought a level of sophistication to our pre-order feature that truly reflects the Harrods brand. The result exceeded our expectations — customers love the seamless experience, and our team has seen a dramatic reduction in manual processing."

— James Richardson, Senior Product Manager, Harrods Digital
